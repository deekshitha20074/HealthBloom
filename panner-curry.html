Calorie Check - Paneer Curry

Nutritional Values - 1 Cup of Paneer Curry

  • Calories: 300-350
  • Carbohydrates: 12-15 grams
  • Protein: 15-20 grams
  • Fat: 20-25 grams
  • Fiber: 2-3 grams
  • Sodium: 500-600 mg

Tips for Healthy Consumption

  • Choose Low-Fat Paneer: Opt for low-fat paneer to reduce the fat content.
  • Minimize Cream Usage: Replace cream with yogurt or cashew paste for a healthier base.
  • Cook with Less Oil: Use minimal oil while preparing the curry.
  • Add Vegetables: Enhance nutrition by adding spinach, peas, or bell peppers.
  • Control Portions: Limit to one serving to manage calorie intake.
