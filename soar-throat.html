Sore Throat: Seasonal Remedies

What is it?

A sore throat is a painful, dry, or scratchy feeling in the throat, often caused by infections, dry air, or seasonal allergies.

Quick Remedies

  1. Gargle with Salt Water: Mix 1/2 teaspoon of salt in warm water and gargle to soothe irritation.
  2. Honey and Warm Water: Drink a mixture of warm water and honey to reduce inflammation and discomfort.
  3. Stay Hydrated: Keep your throat moist by drinking plenty of fluids.
  4. Herbal Teas: Try chamomile or ginger tea for natural relief.

When to Seek Help

Consult a doctor if the sore throat lasts more than a week or is accompanied by a high fever or difficulty swallowing.
