Body Odor: Seasonal Remedies

What is it?

Body odor occurs when sweat interacts with bacteria on the skin, often worsened by heat or poor hygiene.

Quick Remedies

  1. Maintain Hygiene: Bathe regularly with antibacterial soap to reduce bacteria buildup.
  2. Use Antiperspirants: Apply antiperspirants or deodorants to control sweating and odor.
  3. Wear Breathable Fabrics: Opt for cotton or moisture-wicking clothes to keep your skin dry.
  4. Monitor Diet: Avoid spicy foods and garlic, which can contribute to stronger odors.

When to Seek Help

Consult a doctor if body odor persists despite good hygiene, as it may indicate an underlying medical condition.
