Dandruff: Quick Guide

What is it?

Dandruff is a common scalp condition that causes flaking and itching of the skin on the scalp.

Common Causes

  • Dry scalp
  • Oily skin or hair
  • Fungal infections
  • Stress or hormonal changes

Quick Tips to Reduce Dandruff

  1. Use Anti-Dandruff Shampoo: Opt for shampoos with zinc pyrithione, salicylic acid, or ketoconazole.
  2. Keep Your Scalp Clean: Wash your hair regularly to prevent oil buildup.
  3. Moisturize: Apply coconut oil or aloe vera to hydrate your scalp.
  4. Limit Hair Products: Avoid overusing styling products that can irritate the scalp.
  5. Healthy Diet: Include foods rich in omega-3 fatty acids and vitamins to support scalp health.

When to Seek Help

If dandruff persists or worsens, consult a dermatologist for targeted treatment.
