Dark Circles: Quick Guide

What are they?

Dark circles are discolorations or shadows under the eyes, often caused by tiredness, aging, or genetics.

Common Causes

  • Lack of sleep
  • Stress
  • Dehydration
  • Allergies or genetics

Quick Tips to Reduce Dark Circles

  1. Get Enough Sleep: Aim for 7-8 hours of quality sleep each night.
  2. Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water to maintain skin elasticity.
  3. Use a Cold Compress: Apply chilled cucumber slices or cold tea bags to reduce puffiness.
  4. Moisturize: Use an under-eye cream with hyaluronic acid or vitamin C.
  5. Healthy Diet: Incorporate foods rich in antioxidants, like berries and leafy greens.

When to Seek Help

If dark circles persist despite remedies, consult a dermatologist for advanced treatments.
