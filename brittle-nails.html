Brittle Nails: Quick Guide

What are they?

Brittle nails are nails that break, split, or peel easily due to a lack of moisture, nutrients, or excessive exposure to harsh chemicals.

Common Causes

  • Frequent handwashing or exposure to water
  • Low vitamin intake, especially biotin and zinc
  • Harsh chemicals in cleaning products or nail polish removers
  • Underlying health conditions like thyroid issues

Quick Tips to Strengthen Nails

  1. Moisturize Regularly: Use hand creams or oils to keep nails hydrated.
  2. Take Biotin Supplements: Biotin helps improve nail strength and growth.
  3. Avoid Harsh Chemicals: Protect nails by wearing gloves when handling cleaning products.
  4. Trim Nails Regularly: Keeping nails trimmed reduces the chance of splitting.
  5. Use a Nail Strengthener: Apply strengthening nail polishes or oils that promote healthier nails.

When to Seek Help

If your nails remain brittle despite at-home care, or if you notice severe changes in color or texture, consult a dermatologist or healthcare professional.
