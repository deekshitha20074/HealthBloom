Calorie Check - Biryani

Nutritional Values - 1 Cup of Chicken Biryani

  • Calories: 290-350
  • Carbohydrates: 45-50 grams
  • Protein: 15-20 grams
  • Fat: 8-12 grams
  • Fiber: 2-4 grams
  • Sodium: 500-700 mg

Tips for Healthy Consumption

  • Use Brown Rice: Replace white rice with brown rice for added fiber and nutrients.
  • Portion Control: Limit to 1 cup per meal to avoid overeating.
  • Use Lean Protein: Opt for chicken or turkey over red meat to reduce fat content.
  • Minimize Oil: Use minimal oil or replace it with healthier options like olive oil.
  • Add Vegetables: Incorporate vegetables like peas, carrots, or beans for added nutrition.
