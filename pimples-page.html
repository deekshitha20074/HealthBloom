Pimples: Quick Guide

What are they?

Pimples occur when skin pores get clogged with oil, dead skin, and bacteria.

Common Causes

  • Hormonal changes
  • Oily skin
  • Stress
  • Poor skincare habits

Quick Tips to Manage Pimples

  1. Cleanse Gently: Use a mild cleanser twice daily to remove excess oil.
  2. Spot Treatment: Apply tea tree oil, benzoyl peroxide, or salicylic acid to affected areas.
  3. Stay Hydrated: Drink water to keep your skin balanced.
  4. Avoid Touching: Keep your hands off your face to prevent bacteria spread.
  5. Healthy Diet: Reduce sugary and oily foods.

When to Seek Help

If pimples are severe or persistent, consult a dermatologist.
