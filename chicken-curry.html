Calorie Check - Chicken Curry

Nutritional Values - 1 Cup of Chicken Curry

  • Calories: 250-300
  • Carbohydrates: 10-15 grams
  • Protein: 20-25 grams
  • Fat: 10-15 grams
  • Fiber: 2-3 grams
  • Sodium: 500-600 mg

Tips for Healthy Consumption

  • Use Lean Chicken: Opt for skinless chicken breast to reduce fat content.
  • Minimize Cream: Replace heavy cream with yogurt or coconut milk for a healthier alternative.
  • Control Oil: Use minimal oil while preparing the curry to keep it light.
  • Add Vegetables: Include vegetables like spinach, tomatoes, or bell peppers for added nutrition.
  • Spice Wisely: Use fresh spices and avoid store-bought spice mixes high in sodium.
