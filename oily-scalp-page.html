Oily Scalp: Quick Guide

What is it?

An oily scalp is caused by the overproduction of sebum, leading to greasy hair and sometimes an itchy scalp.

Common Causes

  • Overwashing or excessive hair washing
  • Hormonal imbalances
  • Using heavy or unsuitable hair products
  • Diet rich in oily or processed foods

Quick Tips to Manage an Oily Scalp

  1. Avoid Overwashing: Wash hair 2-3 times a week to prevent stimulating excess oil production.
  2. Use Mild Shampoos: Opt for clarifying shampoos specifically designed for oily scalps.
  3. Avoid Heavy Products: Stay away from oily or creamy conditioners and styling products.
  4. Rinse with Cool Water: Use cool water to reduce oil production and close scalp pores.
  5. Healthy Diet: Consume a balanced diet with less greasy or processed foods and more vegetables.

When to Seek Help

If your oily scalp persists or leads to hair loss or dandruff, consult a dermatologist for professional advice.
