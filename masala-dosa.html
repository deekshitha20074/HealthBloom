Calorie Check - Masala Dosa

Nutritional Values - Medium sized Masala Dosa

  • Calories: 250-300
  • Carbohydrates: 30-35 grams
  • Protein: 6-8 grams
  • Fat: 8-10 grams
  • Fiber: 2-3 grams
  • Sodium: 300-400 mg

Tips for Healthy Consumption

  • Choose Low-Oil Cooking: Use non-stick pans or minimal oil to prepare the dosa.
  • Include Vegetables: Add more vegetables to the potato filling for added fiber and vitamins.
  • Opt for Multigrain Batter: Replace rice with millets or quinoa for a healthier twist.
  • Portion Control: Limit to one masala dosa per meal to balance calorie intake.
  • Pair with Healthy Sides: Enjoy with sambhar and coconut chutney for a balanced meal.
