Sunburn: Seasonal Remedies

What is it?

Sunburn is skin damage caused by overexposure to the sun's ultraviolet (UV) rays, resulting in redness, pain, and peeling.

Quick Remedies

  1. Cool the Skin: Apply a cold compress or take a cool bath to soothe the affected area.
  2. Moisturize: Use aloe vera gel or a gentle moisturizer to hydrate and heal the skin.
  3. Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water to help your skin recover and avoid dehydration.
  4. Avoid Further Exposure: Stay out of the sun until the burn heals to prevent worsening the damage.

When to Seek Help

Seek medical attention if blisters cover a large area, you experience severe pain or fever, or signs of infection appear.
