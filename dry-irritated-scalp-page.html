Dry and Irritated Scalp: Quick Guide

What is it?

A dry and irritated scalp is often caused by a lack of moisture, leading to itching, flaking, and discomfort.

Common Causes

  • Excessive use of harsh shampoos or hair products
  • Cold or dry weather conditions
  • Over-washing or infrequent moisturizing
  • Underlying conditions like eczema or dandruff

Quick Tips to Soothe a Dry Scalp

  1. Moisturize Regularly: Use a scalp-specific moisturizer or oil to hydrate the scalp.
  2. Use Gentle Shampoos: Opt for mild, sulfate-free shampoos that won't strip natural oils.
  3. Limit Hot Water: Wash hair with lukewarm water to avoid drying out the scalp.
  4. Avoid Scratching: Refrain from scratching the scalp, as it can lead to further irritation.
  5. Cover Your Scalp: Wear a hat to protect your scalp from harsh environmental factors.

When to Seek Help

If the dryness and irritation persist or worsen, consult a dermatologist for appropriate treatment.
