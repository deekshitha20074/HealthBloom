Cold: Seasonal Remedies

What is it?

A common cold is a viral infection affecting the upper respiratory tract, often triggered by seasonal changes.

Quick Remedies

  1. Stay Hydrated: Drink warm fluids like herbal teas or soups to soothe your throat and clear congestion.
  2. Steam Inhalation: Inhale steam with eucalyptus oil to relieve nasal congestion.
  3. Rest Well: Get plenty of sleep to support your immune system.
  4. Boost Immunity: Consume foods rich in vitamin C, like oranges and spinach.

When to Seek Help

Consult a doctor if symptoms persist for more than 10 days or if you experience severe discomfort.
