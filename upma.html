Calorie Check - Upma

Nutritional Values - Medium-Sized Serving of Upma

  • Calories: 200-250
  • Carbohydrates: 30-35 grams
  • Protein: 5-7 grams
  • Fat: 6-8 grams
  • Fiber: 2-4 grams
  • Sodium: 300-400 mg

Tips for Healthy Consumption

  • Add Vegetables: Mix in carrots, peas, beans, or spinach to boost fiber and vitamins.
  • Use Less Oil: Prepare upma with minimal oil to keep it low in fat.
  • Incorporate Millets: Replace semolina with millets like ragi or foxtail millet for extra nutrients.
  • Portion Control: Stick to a medium-sized serving to manage calorie intake.
  • Pair with Protein: Add roasted peanuts or a side of yogurt for a protein boost.
