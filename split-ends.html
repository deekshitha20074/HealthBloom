Split Ends: Quick Guide

What are they?

Split ends occur when the hair shaft splits due to damage or lack of moisture, leaving hair frayed or brittle at the tips.

Common Causes

  • Excessive heat styling
  • Lack of regular trims
  • Chemical treatments like coloring or perming
  • Exposure to harsh environmental conditions

Quick Tips to Prevent Split Ends

  1. Trim Regularly: Get your hair trimmed every 6-8 weeks to prevent split ends from worsening.
  2. Minimize Heat Styling: Use heat styling tools sparingly and apply heat protectants beforehand.
  3. Hydrate Your Hair: Use deep conditioning treatments or hair masks weekly to retain moisture.
  4. Be Gentle: Avoid brushing wet hair harshly; use a wide-tooth comb instead.
  5. Protect from Damage: Cover your hair with a hat or scarf when exposed to sunlight or wind.

When to Seek Help

If split ends persist despite care, visit a hairstylist for a professional trim and hair care advice.
