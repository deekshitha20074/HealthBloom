Grey Hair: Quick Guide

What is it?

Grey hair is a natural part of aging, caused by the loss of pigment in hair follicles. However, premature greying can be influenced by genetics, stress, or vitamin deficiencies.

Common Causes

  • Genetics and aging
  • Vitamin deficiencies, especially B12 and folate
  • Stress and emotional strain
  • Hormonal changes

Quick Tips to Manage Grey Hair

  1. Maintain a Balanced Diet: Ensure you get enough vitamins like B12, folate, and iron.
  2. Stress Management: Practice relaxation techniques like yoga or meditation.
  3. Hair Dyes: Use natural or chemical hair dyes to cover grey hair.
  4. Use Natural Oils: Coconut or almond oil can help nourish and strengthen grey hair.
  5. Regular Hair Care: Use gentle shampoos and conditioners to maintain the health of your hair.

When to Seek Help

If you notice rapid or premature greying along with other symptoms, it may be worth consulting a healthcare professional to rule out underlying health issues.
