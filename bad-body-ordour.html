Bad Body Odour: Quick Guide

What is it?

Bad body odour occurs when sweat mixes with bacteria on the skin, causing an unpleasant smell.

Common Causes

  • Excessive sweating
  • Poor hygiene or infrequent showers
  • Diet (spicy foods, garlic, etc.)
  • Hormonal imbalances or stress

Quick Tips to Manage Body Odour

  1. Shower Regularly: Bathe daily to remove sweat and bacteria from the skin.
  2. Use Antiperspirants: Choose a good quality deodorant or antiperspirant to control sweating.
  3. Wear Breathable Fabrics: Choose loose-fitting clothes made from natural fibers like cotton.
  4. Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water to help flush out toxins.
  5. Maintain a Healthy Diet: Avoid excessive spicy foods and opt for a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables.

When to Seek Help

If the odour persists despite good hygiene and lifestyle changes, consult a doctor to rule out underlying medical conditions.
