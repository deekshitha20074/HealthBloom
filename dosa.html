Calorie Check - Dosa

Nutritional Values - Medium-Sized Dosa

  • Calories: 120-150
  • Carbohydrates: 18-20 grams
  • Protein: 3-5 grams
  • Fat: 3-5 grams
  • Fiber: 1-2 grams
  • Sodium: 150-200 mg

Tips for Healthy Consumption

  • Pair with Healthy Sides: Serve dosa with sambhar, coconut chutney, or tomato chutney for a balanced meal.
  • Choose Low-Oil Cooking: Use non-stick pans or minimal oil for a healthier preparation.
  • Incorporate Millets: Experiment with ragi, jowar, or bajra dosa batter for added nutrition.
  • Portion Control: Stick to 1-2 dosas per meal to avoid overeating.
  • Pair with Protein: Add a side of dal or eggs for a protein boost.
