Digestive Issues: Seasonal Remedies

What are they?

Digestive issues include bloating, constipation, or diarrhea, often caused by dietary changes, stress, or seasonal foods.

Quick Remedies

  1. Stay Hydrated: Drink warm water or herbal teas to aid digestion.
  2. Consume Probiotics: Eat yogurt or fermented foods to maintain gut health.
  3. Eat Fiber-Rich Foods: Include whole grains, fruits, and vegetables to prevent constipation.
  4. Avoid Heavy Meals: Opt for smaller, lighter meals to reduce bloating.
  5. Ginger or Peppermint: Use ginger tea or peppermint oil to alleviate nausea or indigestion.

When to Seek Help

If symptoms persist or worsen, such as severe abdominal pain or blood in the stool, consult a healthcare provider immediately.
