Wrinkles: Quick Guide

What are they?

Wrinkles are fine lines and creases on the skin, typically caused by aging and a loss of skin elasticity.

Common Causes

  • Aging and reduced collagen production
  • Sun exposure
  • Smoking
  • Repeated facial expressions
  • Dehydration

Quick Tips to Reduce Wrinkles

  1. Protect Your Skin: Use sunscreen daily to prevent UV damage.
  2. Moisturize: Apply anti-aging creams with retinol, hyaluronic acid, or peptides.
  3. Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of water to keep skin plump.
  4. Avoid Smoking: Quit smoking to preserve skin elasticity.
  5. Healthy Diet: Include antioxidant-rich foods like berries, nuts, and fish.

When to Seek Help

If wrinkles are severe or bothersome, consult a dermatologist for treatments like chemical peels, Botox, or fillers.
