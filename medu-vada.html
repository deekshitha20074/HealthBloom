Calorie Check - Medu Vada

Nutritional Values - Medium-Sized Medu Vada

  • Calories: 150-200
  • Carbohydrates: 20-25 grams
  • Protein: 4-6 grams
  • Fat: 5-8 grams
  • Fiber: 2-3 grams
  • Sodium: 200-300 mg

Tips for Healthy Consumption

  • Use Minimal Oil: Fry medu vada in less oil or opt for baking instead.
  • Pair with Healthy Sides: Serve with sambhar and coconut chutney for a balanced meal.
  • Limit Portions: Stick to 1-2 medu vadas per meal to manage calorie intake.
  • Add Lentils: Incorporate a variety of lentils in the batter for enhanced nutrition.
  • Include Vegetables: Add grated carrots, onions, or spinach for extra fiber.
