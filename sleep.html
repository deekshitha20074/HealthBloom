Get Adequate Sleep: Healthy Habits

Why is it Important?

Sleep is crucial for physical and mental health. It helps restore energy, improve memory, and boost overall well-being.

Benefits of Adequate Sleep

  • Improves concentration and productivity
  • Enhances mood and reduces stress
  • Strengthens the immune system
  • Promotes heart health and balanced hormones

How Much Sleep Do You Need?

Adults should aim for 7-9 hours of sleep per night. Teenagers and children may need more.

Quick Tips for Better Sleep

  1. Stick to a Schedule: Go to bed and wake up at the same time daily.
  2. Create a Sleep-Friendly Environment: Keep your bedroom dark, quiet, and cool.
  3. Avoid Screens Before Bed: Limit exposure to phones or TVs at least an hour before sleep.
  4. Relax Before Bedtime: Practice meditation, deep breathing, or light reading.

When to Seek Help

If you experience prolonged sleep issues like insomnia, consult a doctor or sleep specialist.
